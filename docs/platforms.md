# Platform guides

Pick your platform and follow the execution guide:

- JetStream2 (CPU baseline): platforms/jetstream2/docs/execution.md
- AWS (GPU): platforms/aws/docs/execution.md
- Bridges2 (HPC GPU, Slurm): platforms/bridges2/docs/execution.md
- Delta (HPC GPU, Slurm): platforms/delta/docs/execution.md
- Anvil (HPC GPU, Slurm): platforms/anvil/docs/execution.md

## Evidence (runs)

Completed executions are archived under:

runs/<platform>/<YYYY-MM-DD>/

Examples:
- runs/aws/2026-02-11/
- runs/bridges2/2026-02-16/
- runs/delta/2026-02-23/
- runs/anvil/2026-02-24/
